Building the Best AI-Butler

I am currently focused on developing cutting-edge AI technology to create the most efficient and intelligent AI-Butler. This involves leveraging machine learning algorithms, natural language processing, and advanced automation to assist users in their daily tasks seamlessly. The goal is to build an AI-Butler that can understand user preferences, manage schedules, control smart home devices, and provide real-time assistance, enhancing the overall quality of life.

Open Source and AI Research

My work in open-source and AI research involves contributing to various projects, including reinforcement learning for Large Language Models (LLMs) and creating large-scale datasets. Collaborating with the community has been instrumental in driving innovation and sharing knowledge. One of the significant projects includes solving Tic Tac Toe with an innovative algorithm and utilizing it for reinforced learning in LLMs. Additionally, I co-created a massive VQGAN dataset as an open-source contribution, which has been beneficial for researchers and developers in the field.

Algorithmic Trading & Hedge Fund Experience

During my time at the hedge fund from August 2023 to January 2024, I focused on building a massive simulation capable of testing one million different trading strategies efficiently. This experience honed my mathematical skills and deepened my understanding of algorithmic trading. Although I felt my contributions could have been more impactful, the project reinforced my belief that solving real-world problems is more rewarding than monetary gains.

Entrepreneurial Pursuit

Completing a college degree was never my primary goal; rather, it was the experiences and opportunities I gained during my time at IIT Madras. Post COVID, I was already deeply connected with friends online and was actively involved in building a company with other peers in Bangalore. Strong alumni connections developed while writing "The IITM Nexus" further enabled me to connect with individuals I hadn't previously interacted with. Active participation in the startup ecosystem provided ample opportunities, reducing the necessity for traditional placements. Although access to top labs was something I missed, the dynamic nature of entrepreneurship allowed me to let life flow naturally and explore various interests.

Published 'The IITM Nexus'

Alongside my team at "Insti," we initially enjoyed writing books on college entrepreneurs, sharing their small wins and successes on LinkedIn. One day, alumni reached out, expressing interest in a comprehensive book focusing on IIT Madras Alumni who have founded successful companies. This inspired me to take on the challenge. Writing "The IITM Nexus" was a rollercoaster journey that pushed me from being non-technical to embracing technology, tackling tough questions, managing relationships, and navigating the complexities of closing a company. The book aims to view these entrepreneurs through the lens of current college students, making their stories relatable and inspiring. Reaching #20 on Amazon's top-selling autobiographies was a testament to the book's impact and resonance with readers.

Founded Insti

"Insti" began as a simple campus confession page, which I initially took over when the original admin decided to focus more on studies. What started as a joke—running ads and securing sponsors for the page—quickly grew into a robust media firm. As COVID-19 struck, Insti adapted by expanding its reach beyond the initial college, hiring individuals from over 120 colleges and managing a team of 50 people. We diversified our content beyond memes, offering high-quality posts that resonated with a broader audience. This expansion involved creating multiple sub-companies and executing various initiatives to maintain content quality and manage the growing team effectively. Despite the challenges of managing a large team and limited financial growth, the experience was invaluable in understanding the dynamics of scaling a startup and the importance of adaptability in changing environments.

Joined IIT Madras

Joining IIT Madras in 2018 was a transformative experience that shaped both my personal and professional growth. Here are some of the most significant experiences and projects during my time there:

  • Unexpected Scientist Presentation: I was tasked with preparing a slide presentation on a prominent scientist and their discoveries. To my surprise, during the presentation, the scientist themselves attended and elaborated on the topic. This unexpected event left a lasting impression on both me and my classmates.
  • Innovative T-Shirt Sales: Recognizing that parents feel proud seeing their children at college, my team and I decided to sell T-shirts to parents instead of students. We successfully sold 300 T-shirts within 30 hours, generating a profit of INR 28,000 ($350), despite having minimal design efforts. This venture taught me the importance of identifying unique value propositions and executing them efficiently.
  • "Is Insti Really Safe?" Awareness Campaign: Through my Instagram page, I initiated a series that highlighted real stories of harassment faced by females on campus. This campaign led to the creation of several initiatives aimed at raising awareness and improving campus safety, making a meaningful impact on the community.
